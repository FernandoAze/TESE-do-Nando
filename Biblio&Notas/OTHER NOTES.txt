Visual Display and Retrieval of Music Information
"Interestingly, a recent study[7] provides evidence of the importance of data visualization in music and
shows that, in the context of crowdsourcing music annotations, "more complex audio scenes result in
lower annotator agreement, and spectrogram visualizations are superior in producing higher quality
annotations at lower cost of time and human labor.""
